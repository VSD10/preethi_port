The Challenge

Standing apart in a sea of identical minimalism

Watch enthusiasts get lost in identical minimalist designs or complex technical specs. The goal for Zefora was a brand that feels authoritative and accessible — communicating craftsmanship without intimidation.

The Solution

Precision-crafted for digital luxury

A monochromatic, high-contrast palette — deep midnight blacks and periwinkle accents — paired with scroll-triggered reveals. Users feel the craftsmanship rather than just viewing a catalog. Typography mirrors the watch's dial.

Design Decisions
Typography
Intricate serifs mirroring the watch dial's precision
Color
Deep midnight & cool periwinkle — cold luxury
Animation
Scroll-triggered reveals, subtle parallax depth
Iconography
Hyper-minimalist, geometric — echoes the dial
