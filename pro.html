The Challenge

Solving decision fatigue in travel

Travelers face overwhelming choices when planning trips. The goal for Happy Hours was a brand that feels energetic yet organized — a UI that makes complex tour bookings feel like a simple, joyful journey.

The Solution

Built for clarity & joy

Sophisticated tones and integrated nature icons positioned the brand as a gateway to serene adventures. Every micro-animation was crafted to reduce cognitive load and delight the user at each step.
